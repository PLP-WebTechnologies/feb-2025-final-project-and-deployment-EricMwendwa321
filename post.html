5-Minute Meditation for Beginners

Posted on May 14, 2025 by Admin

Starting your day with mindfulness can set a calm tone for the hours ahead. Even just 5 minutes of meditation can help you feel more centered and aware.

Here's a quick beginner meditation guide:

  • Find a quiet place and sit comfortably
  • Close your eyes and focus on your breath
  • If your mind wanders, gently return to your breath
  • Set a timer for 5 minutes and just breathe

Consistency is key. Try this every morning for one week and see how you feel.
